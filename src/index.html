Lesson 1: Hello world!

In this course we will learn about Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS. This is the most fundamental way to build web sites.

Vanilla means no frameworks or libraries. It's just you and the browser. It's the bare metal of the web and a great way to learn how the web works.

One of the things that makes the web a great platform for developers is how open it is. Under the hood, you can view the HTML, CSS, and JS of any almost any website. In fact, assuming you have access to the developer tools of your browser, you can take a peek at the HTML, CSS, and JS of this website you are on right now.
